About the unit/ Where this unit fits
Trough this unit my pupils will have a deep knowledge about wild animals. They will development important contents about it such as their classification, their diet and the food chain they follow. Work in this unit provides them a whole knowledge of the animal habitats around the world and the characteristics of each one.
We classify animals into different groups according to their characteristics. We describe the most important characteristics of mammals and birds, fish and reptiles, and amphibians and insects, but also we investigate about carnivores, herbivores, omnivoresâ¦
They will also compare the habitats where they live and the conditions it presents.
Finally we will also works with the food chain among animals, their components and how it works within the nature.
Experimental and investigative work focuses on: turning ideas into a form, making and recording observations, searching information in different resources, Draw conclusions, presenting results, Making questions and answers about animals to classify them., Look for information on different means to present it in different ways.
Tasks in this unit will focus on: classify animals depending on different criteria , know the conditions of different habitats and classify the animals in the correct one, describe an animal
In this unit children can use the ICT to investigate and draw up conclusions about their investigations
in the final task my students are going to make an akbum about animals, they use all the information collected during the lessons. in order to develop this main task, thy will work before in different subtask as: animals' cards, posters, mind maps...

Expectations
At the end of this unit all the children must
Identify some habitats in where animals live(desert, ocean, arctic, forest, jungle), distinguish between those which eat plants and those which eat other animals and plants, make a general classification of the animals, have an idea of how a food chain works among the animals, search general information about the different topics we work within the unit.
At the end of this unit most of the children should
Identify some habitats in where animals live and their main features, make the classification of animals according to their main characteristics (mammals,insects, reptiles, amphibias, birds) , search specific information in different resources, and know how a food chain works.
At the end of this unit some of the children could
Identify the habitats and make the classification of animals according to their different conditions and resources, make the classification of different animals according to their main characteristics (reproduction, birth, bloodâ¦), have a deep knowledge of how a food chain works and be able to identify predators and preys in it, have an idea of endangered animals around the world, be able to recognize good resources in order to obtain information for their tasks.
